BAI 05 - Cau hinh JaCoCo va rule >= 80%
=======================================

Bai khong yeu cau giai thich tu luan, chi yeu cau cau hinh. Cac diem cau hinh
da lam:

1) Tich hop jacoco-maven-plugin trong pom.xml.
2) Rule strict trong execution "check":
       <element>BUNDLE</element>
       <counter>LINE</counter>
       <value>COVEREDRATIO</value>
       <minimum>0.80</minimum>
   -> build fail neu line coverage < 80%.
3) Rule chay o pha verify -> workflow goi "mvn -B clean verify" tu kich hoat.
4) actions/upload-artifact luu thu muc target/site/jacoco/ (chua index.html)
   sau khi build, dung if: always() de luu ca khi fail (tien debug).

Coverage thuc te: 5 test bao phu het cac nhanh cua isPalindrome va reverse
-> > 90%, qua nguong 80%.
