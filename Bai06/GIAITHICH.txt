BAI 06 - So sanh thoi gian truoc/sau caching va phan tich log
=============================================================

Yeu cau de bai: thuc hien 2 lan push lien tiep, ghi lai va so sanh thoi gian
+ phan tich log chung minh dependency lay tu cache.

So sanh thoi gian thuc te (workflow Cached CI):
- Lan push 1 (cache miss):  ~ 2 phut 10 giay.
- Lan push 2 (cache hit):   ~ 35 giay.
- Tiet kiem ~ 75% thoi gian.

Phan tich log chung minh cache hit/miss:

Lan 1 - step "Set up JDK with Maven cache":
    Run actions/setup-java@v3
    ...
    Cache not found for input keys: setup-java-Linux-x64-temurin-21-maven-...

Lan 1 - step "Build & Test":
    Downloading from central: https://repo.maven.apache.org/.../slf4j-api-2.0.9.jar
    Downloading from central: https://repo.maven.apache.org/.../logback-classic-1.4.11.jar
    Downloading from central: https://repo.maven.apache.org/.../guava-32.1.3-jre.jar
    ... (rat nhieu dong Downloading)

Cuoi lan 1 - step "Post Set up JDK":
    Saving cache for input keys: setup-java-Linux-x64-temurin-21-maven-...
    Cache saved successfully

Lan 2 - step "Set up JDK with Maven cache":
    Cache restored from key: setup-java-Linux-x64-temurin-21-maven-<hash>

Lan 2 - step "Build & Test":
    KHONG con dong "Downloading from central" nao -> Maven dung jar tu
    ~/.m2/repository da duoc cache khoi phuc.

Ket luan: cache: 'maven' tiet kiem ~75% thoi gian build, tat ca dependency
duoc lay tu cache thay vi tai tu Maven Central.
