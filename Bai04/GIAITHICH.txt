BAI 04 - Refactor cho cross-platform
====================================

Yeu cau de bai: co tinh tao Unit Test xu ly path bang dau cung Windows hoac
Linux, quan sat loi tren OS khong tuong thich, sau do refactor bang
File.separator hoac java.nio.file.Path.

Loi co tinh tao truoc khi sua:
- Test viet:  assertEquals("logs/app.log", path.toString());
- Tren Linux/macOS: PASS (separator '/').
- Tren Windows: FAIL - thuc te tra ve "logs\\app.log".
  Log: expected: <"logs/app.log"> but was: <"logs\app.log">

Refactor da ap dung:
- Code chinh dung Paths.get("logs", "app.log") thay vi noi chuoi tay
  -> JVM tu chon separator phu hop voi OS.
- Test dung Path.endsWith(Path.of("logs", "app.log")) thay vi so chuoi
  -> ket qua dung tren ca 3 OS trong matrix (ubuntu, windows, macos).

Sau khi refactor: 3 job trong matrix deu xanh.
